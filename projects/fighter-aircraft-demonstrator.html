01Project overview

Structural definition that survives both analysis and the factory floor.

My work sat at the interface between aircraft geometry, structural performance, engineering definition and manufacturing. The goal was not simply a finished CAD model, but design information that could support verification, release and production.

Component design backed by analysis and clear engineering definition.

Structural Verification
