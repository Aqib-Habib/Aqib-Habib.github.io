Research

Catching faults from the Residuals.

My thesis work compares observer-based residual-generation strategies for quadrotor actuator faults — under nominal, faulty and parametric-uncertainty conditions.

FAULT DETECTION & ISOLATION
Semester Thesis

Comparative observer-based actuator FDI for quadrotor UAVs under parametric uncertainty

A. Habib · Institute of Flight System Dynamics, Technical University of Munich
→ Targeting IFAC SAFEPROCESS 2027

Luenberger, Thau, EKF and UKF observers are evaluated as residual generators for partial and complete actuator loss-of-effectiveness. The study characterises detection sensitivity, robustness and false-alarm behaviour, with an uncertainty analysis that bounds achievable false-alarm rates as the standout contribution.

Google Scholar →
  • Luenberger

    Linear observer baseline

    Fast, simple residual generation around a trim point — the reference for sensitivity comparisons.
  • Thau

    Nonlinear observer

    Stability guarantees for the nonlinear quadrotor model under Lipschitz conditions.
  • EKF

    Extended Kalman filter

    Stochastic residuals with noise-aware thresholds for realistic measurement conditions.
  • UKF

    Unscented Kalman filter

    Better handling of strong nonlinearity during aggressive fault transients.
